My projects.

The Reef Retreat Hotel Management System (RRHMS) is a project developed by Group 45 for CMPG 223. The system aims to manage various hotel operations, including staff, staff roles, guests, and reservations. It also handles check-ins/check-outs and processes payments. Key features of the RRHMS include:

  • Data Maintenance: Ability to view and modify guest and staff information.
  • Reporting: Provides extensive reports on reservation history, guest information, staff, staff roles, top 10 most successful weeks, and income per time period.
  • Database Structure: The system utilizes a relational database with tables for Staff Roles, Staff, Rooms, Guests, and Reservation, as depicted in the Physical Data Model and ER Diagram.
  • User Interface: The system includes a login form, homepage, and dedicated forms for reservations, staff management, staff roles, hotel rooms, guest information, and reports.
